Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 7, 2025

Introduction

The Catholic Diocese of Enugu ("Diocese", "we", "us", or "our"), through its Directorate of Innovation, operates the CDERegistry.org.ng website, which serves as a platform for recording and managing ecclesiastical data (including parish census information and sacramental records such as birth, baptism, confirmation, first communion, marriage, and death registries). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information.

This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect through the Site, how we use and protect that data, who has access to it, and your rights regarding your personal information. The policy applies to all users of the Site, including clergy, parish officers, and members of the public who submit or access data for sacramental or administrative purposes.

Purpose of Data Collection

We collect and process personal data for clear and specific purposes related to the mission and operations of the Catholic Diocese of Enugu. The primary purposes include:

  • Ecclesiastical Record-Keeping: To record, update, and maintain sacramental records (e.g. baptisms, confirmations, marriages, etc.) and other church-related registries or census data.
  • Service Provision: To provide you with the services of the Site, such as allowing authorized users to input new records, search or verify existing records, and request official extracts or certificates of sacramental records.
  • Communication: To contact individuals when necessary regarding their entries or requests.
  • Statistical and Planning Purposes: To compile anonymized statistics about sacramental trends or parish demographics that help the Diocese in pastoral planning and resource allocation.

We will not use your personal data for purposes unrelated to the above without obtaining your consent, unless otherwise required or permitted by law. We do not use the data collected through this Site for any commercial marketing or advertising purposes.

Types of Data Collected

The types of personal information we collect through CDERegistry.org.ng depend on your interactions with the Site and the services you use. This may include:

  • Personal Identification Information: such as full name, date of birth, gender, and contact details
  • Sacramental and Ecclesiastical Details: information specific to church records
  • Parish Membership and Census Data
  • Account Credentials (for authorized users)
  • Communications
  • Technical Data (Cookies and Usage)

We do not intentionally collect sensitive personal data outside of what is necessary for ecclesiastical purposes. We also do not collect or process financial information through this Site.

Data Security Measures

We understand the importance of safeguarding the personal and ecclesiastical information stored in our systems. The Diocese takes the security of your data seriously and implements a variety of measures to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction of personal data. Our security practices include:

  • Secure Hosting with encryption
  • Access Controls based on roles
  • Password Protection with encryption
  • Employee/Staff Training
  • Data Encryption and Backup
  • Monitoring and Testing
  • Third-Party Security vetting

Your Rights and Choices

We are committed to upholding your rights regarding the personal data we collect and process. Depending on applicable law (including Nigeria's data protection regulations) and consistent with church policies, you have the following rights with respect to your personal data:

  • Right to Access
  • Right to Rectification
  • Right to Deletion (with exceptions for sacramental records)
  • Right to Restrict Processing
  • Right to Object
  • Right to Data Portability
  • Right to Withdraw Consent

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at Contact@CDERegistry.org.ng.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update or revise this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. When we do so, we will change the "Effective Date" at the top of this Policy.

Contact Information

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or the personal data we hold about you, please contact us at:

Directorate of Innovation
Catholic Diocese of Enugu
